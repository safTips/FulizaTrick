What's Fuliza?

Fuliza is an overdraft service that NCBA bank offers through Mpesa. The service allows you to complete MPESA transactions, including sending money and paying bills, when you don’t have enough money in your MPESA wallet.

Need a higher Fuliza Limit?

Safaricom reviews Fuliza limits after three months. Using the service frequently may grow your limit, but after 3 months. But some emergencies may not wait that long.
